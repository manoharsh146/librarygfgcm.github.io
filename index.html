About

Mission & Facilities

Our Mission

The Library & Information Centre fosters academic excellence by enabling equitable access to credible information resources and by cultivating information literacy among students and faculty. We align with Tumkur University curricula and DCE policies to serve the local community.

Reading Spaces

Quiet reading hall, reference section, newspaper corner, and group study tables.

Connectivity

High‑speed campus Wi‑Fi, OPAC access points, and computer terminals for search and e‑learning.

Access & Inclusion

Open stacks, help‑desk support, and priority assistance for learners who need it.

Services

What we offer

Circulation

Issue, return, and renewals as per library rules; overdue SMS/email alerts.

Reference & Guidance

Assistance with finding books, citations, and credible academic sources.

Information Literacy

Workshops on search strategies, plagiarism awareness, and citation managers.

Inter‑library Support

Facilitated access through DCE/University networks for hard‑to‑find materials.

Question Papers & Syllabi

Access previous years' question papers for reference and preparation.

Open Question Papers

NDLI Club Activities

Reading challenges, webinars, and digital literacy events.
